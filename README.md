# Needle & Tide - Shopify E-commerce Website

[Visit Live Site](https://needleandtide.com)

## Overview

**Needle & Tide** is a lifestyle e-commerce brand that required a fully customised Shopify storefront to showcase its products with a premium user experience. I collaborated directly with the brand to design, develop, and implement a website that aligns with their visual identity and business goals.

This project demonstrates my expertise in Shopify development, front-end engineering with Liquid, and building scalable, visually appealing e-commerce experiences.

## My Role

- Developed a **custom Shopify theme** tailored to Needle & Tide’s brand identity.
- Implemented advanced **Liquid coding** to customise product pages, collections, and dynamic content sections.
- Integrated **Shopify apps and custom scripts** for enhanced functionality.
- Optimised **performance, responsiveness, and UX** for desktop and mobile platforms.
- Collaborated with the client to ensure the website reflected their branding and conversion goals.

## Tech Stack

- **Platform:** Shopify  
- **Languages:** Liquid, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript  
- **Frameworks/Libraries:** Shopify Polaris (where applicable), jQuery for interactive elements  
- **Tools:** Git, Shopify CLI, Theme Kit, Figma (design collaboration)

## Key Features

- Fully **custom Shopify theme** from scratch
- Dynamic **product and collection pages** with custom Liquid templates
- Mobile-first **responsive design**
- Integrated **custom promotional banners** and CTA sections
- Optimized **checkout experience** for higher conversions
- SEO-friendly structure with fast-loading pages

## Challenges & Solutions

- **Challenge:** Implementing complex, dynamic product filters and custom sections not available in the base theme.  
  **Solution:** Leveraged advanced Liquid loops, conditionals, and metafields to build a flexible and scalable solution.  

- **Challenge:** Ensuring consistent brand identity across multiple device sizes.  
  **Solution:** Adopted a mobile-first responsive design approach and tested extensively across devices.

## Outcome

The site successfully launched with a polished user experience, reflecting Needle & Tide’s branding and boosting online engagement. The client reported positive feedback from customers regarding ease of navigation and overall design.
